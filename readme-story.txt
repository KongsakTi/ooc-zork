# Story
  - In the well known temple of Xiao lin monks, there is a purification tradition that ones must
  go through in order to achieve the highest honor as xiao lin.
  As the wise one has said "For one to defeat his enemy, one must defeat the evils in his heart".
  This is the story of the xiao lin monk whose achievement will reckon the world !


# Objective
  - Eliminate all the monster on each level to proceed to the next one
